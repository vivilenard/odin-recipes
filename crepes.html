Crepes

Description

This is a sweet and short crepe recipe. Have fun :)

Ingredients

what you will need:

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 cups of (plant)milk
  • one egg or banana
  • one packet of vaniila sugar
  • some sugar
  • a little bit of salt

Steps

  1. put all the ingredients together and mix them
  2. if add more flour or milk until you get the consistency you want
  3. always add a thin layer of dough into your pan until the crepe is good
